About DRR

Rtr Harish Vasan

DRR 2019 - 2020

1920rotaract3000@gmail.com

Rtr. Harish Vasan was born on 26 th October 1991 as only child to his parents Sree. Vasan and Smt. Revathi (late). His father is a caterer. His mother passed away in the year 2008. He completed his schooling from Boys higher secondary school , srirangam. He graduated B.com from Srimad Andavan Arts and Science college in 2013.

He is the proprietor of SREE SASTHA CATERING. He is a share holder of NEWGEN RO PURIFIER. He is one of the MANAGING DIRECTOR of “WE DO” GROUP OF COMPANIES which owns:

  • WE DO Event Management
  • WE DO Agency
  • WE DO Travels
  • WE DO Man power supplies
  • WE DO System sales and services
  • WE DO Health Care Product

Mr.Harish Vasan joined the ROTARY movement (INTERACT) since his school days (2008-2010). He involved himself in ROTARACT as a member form 2010-2011. He took charge as Secretary of Rotaract club of Srimad Aandavan Arts & science college. As the passion cling to his heart so closely, he took initiative and started a new club and took charge as charter President of Rotaract club of Temple City. He successfully completed his presidential period. In that period he received the Best Charter Club Award, Presidential Citation Award and Outstanding Project Award by Rotary International District 3000.

He hit the road into the District as District Secretay Programme 2013 - 2014. From the year 2013-2018 he served as a club coordinator and motivated his club members and also supported the club like a backbone.

He also performed as a Zonal Rotaract Secretary of RID-3000 for the year 2015-2016.
