About DRR

Rtr Harish Vasan

DRR 2019 - 2020

1920rotaract3000@gmail.com

Rtr. Harish Vasan was born on 26 th October 1991 as an only child to his parents Vasan and Smt. Revathi (late). His father is a caterer. His mother passed away in the year 2008. He completed his schooling from Boys higher secondary school , srirangam. He graduated B.com from Srimad Andavan Arts and Science college in 2013.

He is the proprietor of SREE SASTHA CATERING. He is known for his multi tasking ability and compatibility to be a good leader and also a great hard worker. His persistence working made him a idol of what he is now. As an adding note he is also a share holder of NEWGEN RO PURIFIER. He is one of the MANAGING DIRECTOR of “WE DO” GROUP OF COMPANIES which owns:

  • WE DO Event Management
  • WE DO Agency
  • WE DO Travels
  • WE DO Man power supplies
  • WE DO System sales and services
  • WE DO Health Care Product

Apart from the above mentioned qualities he involves himself in much of helping others with his social works. He has a very futuristic thinking that , during his school days itself he joined rotary movement (2008-2010). Since from that minute He involved himself in INTERACT. Mr.Harish Vasan found rotary to be a great platform for his business and social work cordially he also become the a member of ROTARY from 2010-2011. He took charge as Secretary of Rotaract club of Srimad Aandavan Arts & science college. He involves himself completely to it and he has aced it. As this continues the passion towards rotaract cling to his heart so closely. Rotaract is always something which can't be taught or forced and it evoke from inside . This happened excatly to Mr. Harish and he took his own initiative and started a new club and took charge as charter President of Rotaract club of Temple City. Though he had many hurdles in initial stages but still he successfully completed his presidential period. It's is said to get the 1st leaf out of a plant it the seed has to break itself. And this suits completely to Mr. Harish as he was the one who sow the seed of Rotaract club and the thought of Rotaract in many young minds. As a honour of appreciation he wad awarded with Best Charter Club Award, Presidential Citation Award and Outstanding Project Award by Rotary International District 3000.

He hit the road into the District as District Secretay Programme 2013 - 2014. From the year 2013-2018 he served as a club coordinator and motivated his club members and also supported the club immensely.

He performed as a Zonal Rotaract Secretary of RID-3000 for the year 2015-2016

He performed the role of District Secretary Admin during the period 2016-2017.

He waited in the wings from the year 2017-2019. He was elected as DISTRICT ROTARACT REPRESENTATIVE NOMINEE (DRRN) for the year 2017-2018. With full of the joys of springs, he started to build his empire in his elect period (2018-2019).

He will spread his wings along with his “TEAM GARUDA” on June 30th 2019.
